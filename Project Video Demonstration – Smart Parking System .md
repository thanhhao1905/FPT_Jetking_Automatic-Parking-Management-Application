# Smart Parking System 
This project demonstrates the design and implementation of a Smart Parking System, focusing on the integration of license plate recognition, RFID authentication, and automated gate control for vehicle entry and exit.

The video below presents the complete operation of the system, including the camera-based plate detection, RFID verification process, hardware interaction, and the overall real-time performance of the parking management system.
